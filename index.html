What is Overwatch?

Overwatch was released in 2016, and Overwatch 2 was released in 2022. Both games feature hero-based combat between two teams of players vying over various objectives, along with other traditional gameplay modes. Released in 2016, the first video game in the series lacked a traditional story mode.

Hero-based Gameplay

Features a diverse roster of heroes, each with unique abilities, classified into Damage, Tank, and Support roles.

Team-oriented Objectives

Emphasizes cooperation to complete objectives like capturing points or escorting payloads.

Dynamic Maps

Offers a variety of global locations with unique layouts, encouraging different strategies and hero choices.

Stylized Art and Storytelling

Known for its vibrant art style and engaging lore explored through animated shorts and comics.
